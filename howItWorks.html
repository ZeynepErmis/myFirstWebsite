HOW IT WORKS?

rent a bike in just 4 steps to have fun.

Details of Steps


Instant Booking

  • Customers can make reservations when requested.
  • Customers must have a membership to be able to book. If you are not a registered customer, you can register in a short time.
  • If customer have questions about how to sing up, you can call customer service or contact us via e-mail .
  • Compare bike types, locations, prices then choose whichever suits you best.
  • All our bikes come with insurance included.
  • The price of bicycles/bicycles to be rented must be paid in this section.
  • BikEco only accept cards due to corona restrictions but we might accept cash in special cases. The situation should be explained, after that individuals have to email us for this.

Pick up your BikEco

  • There are 8 available BikEco stations in London, customers should book the bike from the station where want to get it.
  • We have authorized employees at every station. If there is something you do not like about the bike or something wrong, our qualified and friendly employees will help you and the problem will be solved in a short time.

Enjoy your ride

  • Helmets will be provided to you at the stations by the authorities. You have to use a helmet for your safety/health.
  • Remember you have a Hop-on/ Drop off chance!
  • Make sure you ready for a nice ride with us!

Park

  • Parking is one of the most important issues for us. For a high quality ride, each individual should use the bicycle carefully.
  • Our stations are open 24/7, protected by security guards and cameras for security.
  • You can pick up one of our 8 stations whichever suits you best.
